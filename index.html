ABOUT ME

Grace (Xinyue) Ji

Architecture Major at Carnegie Mellon University

Grace is an Architecture student at Carnegie Mellon University pursuing a five-year Bachelor degree in Architecture. She has a bilingual culture background. Grace have travelled to many parts of the the wrorld and enjoyed experiencing different culture. She thinks it gives her inspiration to art, architecture and life. She is also a Chinese calligrapher.

Tillie Collage

This collage derives from the Tillie Speyer House study. The collage is blue tone based, which converys information and details about the buildning as well as the visual pleasure.

Twisting

Motion study of two tools results in the production of this model. It expresses the sense of twisting, drilling, enclosing and transition in its form and structure. Oak and poplar were used.

Descending

This is a architectual spatial study of perspective cube drawing. The purpose is to understand how to create space by taking out different pieces. The drawing was then reversed in color for design purpose.
